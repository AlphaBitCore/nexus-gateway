# Community Code Of Conduct

Nexus Gateway operates under a Code of Conduct adapted from the Contributor Covenant v2.1. The full text is in [`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](https://github.com/AlphaBitCore/nexus-gateway/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) at the repository root. This page summarizes the standards and explains where to report violations.

---

## Standards

All participants in Nexus Gateway community spaces — issues, pull requests, Discussions, commit messages, and any public representation of the project — are expected to uphold the following standards.

**Behavior that builds a productive community:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Staying focused on what is best for the community.
- Demonstrating empathy toward other community members.

**Behavior that is not acceptable:**

- Personal attacks, insulting or derogatory comments, or trolling.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

The Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces where contributors interact on behalf of the project. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces (conferences, social media, etc.).

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent to the maintainers at **security@alphabitcore.com**. This is the same address used for security vulnerability reports; emails to this address are treated as confidential by default.

All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly. The privacy and security of the reporter are respected throughout the process. Possible outcomes range from a private warning to a temporary or permanent ban from community spaces, depending on the severity and history of the behavior.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face repercussions as determined by other members of project leadership.

## Attribution

The Nexus Gateway Code of Conduct adapts portions of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1. For the canonical text and detailed enforcement guidelines, see the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
